Software

As my operating system, I mainly use Gentoo GNU/Linux since I like Gentoo being quite easy to understand and I like portage a lot. Bonus is of course the rolling release architecture.
My IDE/text editor is Neovim.
For browsing the interwebs, I use qutebrowser.
I use BSPWM and DWM as my window manager(s) on the X window system.
I use the ZSH shell with ohmyz.sh.
For accessing privacy unfriendly services I use my own instances/frontends.

Devices

Laptop

My laptop is a Lenovo ThinkPad X280 (i5-8350U, 16GB, 1TB NVMe, 1080p touch). I do hate Intel ME, but not enough to switch to a T60 or something from "that era". I don't "need more" than a T60 actually, but I do want a laptop which has good battery life, is thin and light and has a nice (touch!) panel.

PC

My PC is custom built (Ryzen 5 5600X, RTX 2060, 24GB 3000MHz, 500GB SSD). I use it mainly for gaming and thus it sadly has Windows and Gentoo dual booted on it. Since I have a good firewall (on my laptop and network, physical), I do sort of trust Windows a little more.
